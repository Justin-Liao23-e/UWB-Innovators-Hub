Our Mission

We want to foster a community where ambition, passion, and innovation come together. By offering resources, support, and a network of like-minded peers, we help bring bold ideas to life, resolving real-world problems while creating impactful values to the world.

Join us through our Discord server (please complete the short 1-min onboarding process).

What We Provide

  • Discussion Forum: Engage in lively talks during our entrepreneurial and networking events to learn and connect with startup founders, industry speakers, and student innovators.
  • Events: Attend private company tours (Expedia, Amazon, etc.), local tech conferences and hackathons, and casual gym hangouts for exclusive experiences beyond campus.
  • Workshops: Enhance your skills with industry leaders in software engineering, law, design, and business, continuously acquiring knowledge across diverse industries.
  • Launchpad: Our 8-week accelerator/incubator program takes you from ideation to launch, offering mentorship and workshops until the Demo Day, where you can pitch to investors.
